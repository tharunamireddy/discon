Submission Guidelines for DISC 2024

Overview

Authors are invited to submit original, unpublished research papers to DISC 2024. Submissions should align with the conference themes and follow the guidelines below.

Submission Process

  1. Preparation:
    • Format: Use the provided LaTeX/Word template to format your paper.
    • Length: Maximum 6 to 7 pages, including references.
    • Language: All submissions must be in English.
  2. Online Submission:
    • Platform: Submit your paper through the Microsoft Conference Management Tool.
    • Account: Create an account or log in to the submission portal.
  3. Paper Details:
    • Title and Abstract: Enter the paper title and abstract in the submission system.
    • Keywords: Provide up to five keywords that describe your paper.
    • Authors: List all authors and their affiliations.
